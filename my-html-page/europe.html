Europe Adventures

My grandpa was a very adventurous man and had so many different life experiences. When I was 20, he asked me if I wanted to go to Europe for several months with him. I was struggling in college at the time and jumped at the offer. We originally lived in Madrid, Spain for a month where we rented a cute apartment and explored the city. After that my mom joined us for our travels and we explored the country for another month. My family offered that I could continue in Europe for another several months by myself and while it was a bit scary to solo travel in a different continent, I am so glad I took the opportunity. I traveled to Southern France, Norway, The Netherlands, Germany, the Czech Republic. After 3 months of traveling I volunteered at a hostel in Romania for several weeks. I was planning on continuing my travels, but unfortunately my grandpa was diagnosed with stage 4 cancer. I immediately flew home to spend his last few months together but I was so grateful for the adventures we had when he was still well. He was always such an inspiration and helped facilitate my passion for adventure.
